## COPD Phenotype Discovery

This project applies unsupervised random-forest proximity clustering and partitioning around medoids to identify potential COPD-related participant phenotypes. The workflow includes missing-data assessment, median and random-forest imputation, silhouette analysis, cluster comparison, and permutation-based variable importance.

**Tools:** R, randomForest, cluster, rfImpute, rfPermute, Quarto
